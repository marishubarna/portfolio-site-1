10 Best Manga of All Time

© By Shuniaieva Marianna.Published 2023.05.02

Manga (漫画, [maŋga][a]) are comics or graphic novels originating from Japan. Most manga conform to a style developed in Japan in the late 19th century, and the form has a long history in earlier Japanese art. The term manga is used in Japan to refer to both comics and cartooning. Outside of Japan, the word is typically used to refer to comics originally published in the country. In Japan, people of all ages and walks of life read manga .The medium includes works in a broad range of genres: action, adventure, business and commerce, comedy, detective, drama, historical, horror, mystery, romance, science fiction and fantasy, erotica (hentai and ecchi), sports and games, and suspense, among others. Many manga are translated into other languages.

  • One Piece

    • A huge number of manga fans all over the globe agree that One Piece is thead best manga of all time. The action, adventure manga series, written by Eiichiro Oda, has been serialized as a main focus of Weekly Shonen Jump since the first episode was released in 1997. The manga series consisting of around 100 volumes has been also adapted into an anime series since 1999 and lots of anime movies ever. The walking legend with the theme of pirates presents the long adventurous story of the Straw Hat Pirates and its captain Monkey D. Luffy. He voyages in search of the supreme treasure called One Piece and has a dream to become the next King of the Pirates. During the journey across the seas, he meets unique and reliable crews including a swordsman Zoro, a navigator Nami, a cook Sanji and so on. In the manga series, a Devil Fruit is symbolized, which gives a special ability to those who eat one while they come to swim like a stone in exchange. Luffy and many other characters eat various Devil Fruits, who fight with each other using the supernatural powers gifted by the fruits. One Piece is also appraised worldwide since lots of historical and social problems are described in the story including a slavery, discrimination, drugs, etc. The best manga, which is not only categorized as action adventure, also makes readers get impressed, excite and pleased throughout the whole story.

  • Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

    • A newcomer in the 21st century sends a shock wave throughout the manga and anime industry around the world, whose name is Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba (鬼滅の刃). Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, the best modern dark-fantasy manga without a doubt, started its publication on Weekly Shonen Jump 2016 and completed its story in May, 2020. The first season of the anime adaptation was broadcasted in 2019. The smash-hit anime movie Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie: Mugen Train, released in October, 2020, broke the box-office records in Japanese movies. The setting of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a fictional Japan in the Taisho period (1912-1926) and it focuses on a conflict between humans and demons. The story centers on a young boy Tanjiro Kamado, who loses his family that are attacked by demons. His only survived sister Nezuko is also transformed into a demon. He makes a decision to fight against demons as a member of the Demon Slayer Corps. During the adventure, he meets unique friends Zenitsu Agatsuma and Inosuke Hashibira. They continue their journeys to beat the strongest demons group called the Twelve Kizuki and the king of demons named Muzan Kibutsuji with the nine powerful demon slayer masters called the Hashira in union.

  • Naruto

    • Naruto (NARUTO -ナルト-) is one of the reasons why many manga fans come to like a manga and an anime. The Ninja themed manga series was serialized on Weekly Shonen Jump between 1999 and 2014, written by Masashi Kishimoto. The grand story of the manga series was adapted into two titles of an anime series from 2002 to 2017. Naruto is also followed by the sequel Boruto: Naruto Next Generations focusing on the son of Naruto, which has been published since 2016. Naruto is set in a modern Ninja world which is balanced by the Five Great Shinobi (Ninja) Countries. The story follows a young Ninja Naruto living in the hidden leaf village in the Land of Fire. He is never talented in the aspect of study or martial arts. It depicts the grow-up story of the inferior boy, who trains hard and fights against many powerful Ninja to become a Hokage, the leader of the country. Not only the main character but also other attractive Ninja like Sasuke and Sakura are featured in the action adventure manga.

  • Dragon Ball

    • Which manga do you associate with the word manga at first? Many people should say the title of the legendary manga Dragon Ball (ドラゴンボール). The martial-arts adventure manga franchise was started on Weekly Shonen Jump by Akira Toriyama from 1984. The original manga was adapted into several anime adaptations including Dragon Ball and Dragon Ball Z (Dragon Ball Kai is a remake of Dragon Ball Z). Dragon Ball is followed by the sequel written by Akira Toriyama Dragon Ball Super (ドラゴンボール超) from 2015. One of the most famous and popular manga proceeds with an adventure of a young boy Son Goku. He travels around the world in search of the seven Dragon Balls scattered throughout the world, which carry out a wish of the owner. He develops his martial arts skills training and fighting with various characters. His sons Gohan and Goten are focuses on in the second half of the story. What is one of the highlights of Dragon Ball is powerful battle scenes of strong characters using supernatural techniques such as Kamehameha and Spirit Bomb.

  • Hunter x Hunter

    • Hunter x Hunter is the greatest manga written by Yoshihiro Togashi, who is also known for Yu Yu Hakusho. The adventure manga has been serialized on Weekly Shonen Jump since 1998, adapted into two versions of anime series aired in 1999-2001 and 2011-2014. Fortunately, the manga artist re-started writing in May, 2022 since the serialization of the manga stopped in December, 2018 due to the condition of the manga artist. Hunter x Hunter presents a variety of stories of hunters who are licensed members of the Hunter Association and are highly skilled in pursuing secret treasures, epicurean food, bounties, rare animals and so on. A main character Gon Freecss decides to become a licensed hunter to find his missing father Ging Freecss, who is known as a top hunter. There are lots of attractive unique characters including Gon’s friends Kurapika, Killua and Leorio and powerful villains. In addition, the story of tight-knit relationships and even treachery between them are depicted as well as other popular manga on Shonen Jump.

  • Akira

    • Akira is one of the top-rated cyberpunk manga attracting a large number of fans all over the world. It was written by the famous Katsuhiro Otomo consisting of 6 volumes published in 1982-1990. An anime movie adaptation, which is also quite famous worldwide, was directed by himself in 1988 though it is based on a part of the whole story (2-3 volumes of the manga series). Akira is set in a fictional Tokyo called Neo-Tokyo in 2019 in the process of development and recovery from devastation due to World War III. The story proceeds with the two main characters Shotaro Kaneda and Tetsuo Shima, who are biker gangs. They get involves in the conspiracy planned by military, which is related to the key person Akira.

  • Attack on Titan

    • Attack on Titan (進撃の巨人) is looked on as one of the best manga in this modern age, categorized as action and dark fantasy. It began its publication on Bessatsu Shonen Magazine from 2009 and the story is to be completed in April, 2021, finally. An anime adaptation was made for the first time in 2013 and the final season was released in the end of 2020. Attack on Titan depicts people living in the city surrounded by walls and the mystery related to titans that threaten the peace of humanity. A protagonist Eren Yeager and other Survey Corps members fight against the titans and investigate what they are and where they come from.

  • Oshi No Ko

    • Oshi no Ko is a highly acclaimed manga by Akasaka Aka, the renowned author of the romantic comedy series Kaguya-sama, featuring gorgeous artwork by Yokoyari Mengo. The story begins with Ai Hoshino, a talented and beloved 16-year-old idol who is loved by her fans for her beautiful voice and stunning appearance. Ai’s life takes a dramatic turn when she becomes pregnant with twins, which is considered taboo in the idol industry. Despite the risks, Ai is determined to give birth to her children and seeks the help of Goro Amemiya, a skilled gynaecologist living in the countryside who happens to be her biggest fan. Goro agrees to help Ai with her prenatal care, but tragedy strikes on the night of her delivery when he is attacked and killed. However, Goro wakes up to find himself reincarnated as Ai’s son, Aquamarine Hoshino. As Aquamarine, Goro discovers the harsh reality of the entertainment industry, where talent alone is not enough to guarantee success, and many secrets are hidden beneath the glamorous exterior. The manga offers a unique perspective on the world of showbiz and explores themes of motherhood, love, and ambition.

  • Dandadan

    • Dandadan is one of the most bizarre manga you will ever read that will take you on a wild and unpredictable ride. Reading this manga will remind many of you of Chainsaw man which is no surprise since Tatsu Yukinobu the author of Dandadan used to work as an assistant for Tatsumaki Fujimoto. The story revolves around two eccentric high schoolers, Momo Ayase and Okarun, who are fixated on their respective paranormal beliefs. Momo believes in ghosts while Okarun is a firm believer in aliens. When the two get into an argument over which is real, they decide to visit locations associated with both the occult and the supernatural to prove each other wrong. Turns out that neither of them was wrong and that both the aliens and ghosts do exist. With its mix of sci-fi, supernatural, and surreal elements, Dandadan is a manga that will keep you guessing at every turn. Follow Momo and Okarun on their wacky and unpredictable journey as they navigate the strange and mysterious world around them.

  • Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind

    • Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind (風の谷のナウシカ) is more famous as a Studio Ghibli movie than as a manga, however, it was originally made as the manga series by Hayao Miyazaki. Totally, 7 volumes were published between 1982 and 1994, and only a portion of the manga series was adapted into the anime film in 1984. Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind is set in a futuristic and apocalyptic world, which is heavily polluted but humans still inhabit. The story focuses on Nausicaä, who is the princess of the Valley of the Wind and the leader of humanity. She seeks a way that humans and creatures in the Toxic Jungle coexist.
